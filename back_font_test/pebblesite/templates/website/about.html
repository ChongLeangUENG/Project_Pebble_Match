Love is not always at first sight.
Love can be blind,
Love can be cultivated.
However,
Love can also be damaged.
By all the upsetting little things in our different living habits, behaviors, values and so on.
Instead of trying to change all these gaps,
Why not avoid them in the first place?

Pebble Match is a matchmaker website.
There is no judgment on people’s looks.
Pebble Match matches people by a questionnaire on habits and values,
Which is a fundamental (albeit often overlooked) element in a relationship.

Have you noticed that there are some little penguins waddling around on the site?
Yes! They have a story too!
For some species of penguins (Adelie, Chinstrap and Gentoo),
a male penguin searches for the prettier pebble to propose to the penguin of its life.
(after all, humans also propose with a diamond) br If the other penguin likes it, it will bring it back to its nest,
Then the two love birds can build up their pebble nest to raise their family!

  1. You must sign up to create an account, and “Login”.
  2. In your personal page, click “Quiz” to start.
  3. Once you finish the quiz, click on “Match”.
  4. Your top three matches will show up on the page.
  5. You can also find your other matches with their scores on “My Match”.
  6. You have three complementary pebbles to decide who to give a pebble to.
  7. If your match accepts your pebble, then you might have found your match!
  8. Receive a notification in your mailbox whether your pebble has been accepted or rejected.
